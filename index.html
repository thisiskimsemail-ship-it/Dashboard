Apple Mail Privacy Protection inflates email opens
Low Won't fix
Since iOS 15 (Sept 2021), Apple Mail pre-fetches images on inbox load, which HubSpot then registers as an "open" — regardless of whether the recipient actually opened the email. Industry estimates put MPP inflation at 20–40% of total opens. EDM Engagement KPIs (Active 30d = 4,018, Champions, Active readers) are likely overstated by 20–40%.
ContextPlatform-level constraint, not a Wade-side bug — affects every B2B email program. Best practice: weight click data more heavily than opens when deciding who's actually engaged. The Champions segment (click-engaged 30d) is unaffected — clicks aren't inflated by MPP. So treat Champions as the trustworthy hot cohort, Active readers as directional.
